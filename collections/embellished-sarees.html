Types of Embellished sarees

Embellished sarees are often referred to as Swarovski sarees, Stone Work Sarees, or simply embellished sarees. These names reflect the various techniques and materials used to create these stunning garments. Whether it is the sparkle of Swarovski crystals or the intricate work of stones, each embellished saree is a masterpiece that exudes beauty and refinement.

Popular Celebrity Embellished Sarees

Some of the most famous embellished sarees include:

  1. The red Swarovski sarees worn by Deepika Padukone at the 2019 IIFA Awards
  2. The yellow Stone Work Sarees worn by Priyanka Chopra at the 2019 Met Gala
  3. The blue Embellished saree worn by Kareena Kapoor at the 2019 Filmfare Awards
  4. The green Swarovski sarees worn by Alia Bhatt at the 2020 Filmfare Awards
  5. The pink Embellished saree worn by Sonam Kapoor at the 2019 Cannes Film Festival
  6. White Stone Work Sarees worn by Alia Bhatt on her wedding day April 2022

Celebrities like Deepika Padukone, Priyanka Chopra, and Kareena Kapoor have all been spotted in Stone Work Sarees, showcasing their versatility and glamour.

The Making Of Stone Work Sarees

Stone Work Sarees are crafted in various parts of India, primarily in cities like Jaipur, Delhi, and Mumbai. Skilled artisans use traditional techniques to create designs and patterns, often taking several days or even weeks to complete a single saree.
